FAQ

WHAT IS THIS APP


WHAT IS THIS APP

The app tries to solve the fundamental of car pooling i.e. suggesting people who stay near your route, and its upto to person who is offering ride and person taking the ride to communicate and plan the ride.


HOW DO I REGISTER

You authorise with Google and verify using the official email id.


WHY AUTHORISE VIA GOOGLE

Its a better user experience and we dont have build a seperate login module.And its more secure


WHAT DO YOU STORE FROM GOOGLE

Nothing!


CAN I BOOK A RIDE

You dont book a ride, You request for a ride.


WHAT IS DIFFERENCE BETWEEN BOOKING AND REQUESTING

Booking a ride: The app is responsible for all the things that happen
Request a ride: The scope of the app ends after the suggesting you the ride


WHY DONT YOU TAKE RESPONSIBILITY

Developers dont take any responsibility, Thats the universal truth!


WHY DOESNT THE APP ALLOCATE ME RIDE AUTOMATICALLY, WHY JUST SUGGEST

Developers dont own the car, Its upto the consent of the person who is offering the ride.


HOW DO I ENSURE MY FELLOW EMPLOYEE PICKS ME UP AT THE RIGHT TIME AND RIGHT PLACE

You can communicate well in advance and decide on the place and time, The app doesnt doesnt track the rider or the requestor


CAN I TRACK MY RIDE

No! We havent and we will not implement this feature


AS A PERSON OFFERING THE RIDE I WANT THE FUEL COSTS TO BE SHARED, HOW DOES THE APP ENSURE THIS

The app doesnt suggest what to pay and whom to pay.Every car has different fuel efficieny so we cant have fixed price and its not the problem we are trying to solve.


THIS APP IS NO WAY NEAR WHAT WE GET FROM OF MARKET CAR POOLING APPS, WHY IS IT SO

The fundamental problem in car pooling is knowing people who stay near my rotue/home,so we just focus on solving the fundamental problem.
So we dont do tracking,payments, acceptance,rejection, Gender preference,Rating etc etc. More features require more effort and thinking, more bandwidth = more money, and we dont have any budget.
Other apps charge you money and we wont.

SECURITY & PRIVACY

IS THIS APP SECURE

We think so, If you find any flaws please catch hold of the developers


SHOULD I GIVE MY EXACT HOME ADDRESS

No need, nearest landmark would do and whatever you give, we store the destination ~1km away (This was bug in implementation,but later promoted as a feature)


PERFORMANCE

IS THIS APP PERFORMANT, HOW MUCH BANDWIDTH IS CONSUMED

The whole app is less than 500kb,We measure Time to be Interactive as our metric
On a Moto G (1st Gen) and on Slow 3G network these are the results
Page load for the first time takes 3.3 Seconds
Subsequent load takes just 800 milliseconds
This is possible because of Service Workers!

WHAT IS THE TECHNOLOGY STACK & WHAT ARE THE TOOLS USED

The app is built on Firebase.
Front End:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JS
Lamda Funtions:
  • Node.js
Database:

Google Map API's

WHY A WEBSITE, WHY NOT A NATIVE APP

Browsers are very powerful these days, whatever native apps do, browsers can do it better and faster. You need native apps if need is everyday,
Quick check How many apps do you use regularly and how many are just sitting there on the phone?
Trust me you dont need native app Have a look at progressive web apps.
Native apps requires a different skill set which we dont have


HOW CAN YOU SAY, NATIVE APP IS NOT REQUIRED

From user perspective:Native App

  • You go to playstore
  • Search for the app
  • Install the app (which will be more than 5mb)
  • Then use the app
  • The app consumes sufficient memory and battery
  • The old version just sits on the phone
Progressive web app:
  • You visit the URL
  • Start using the App
  • Add to HomeScreen if you need quick access!
